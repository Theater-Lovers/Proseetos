About Proseetos

Our vision is to become the go-to platform for all theatre lovers in Netherlands, welcoming everyone, regardless of their needs or abilities. We aim to create a ritual: a single, inclusive website where people check before every theatre visit. By gathering all performances in one accessible, easy-to-use, and thoughtfully designed space, we ensure that no one must search multiple websites to find the perfect show.

Values

Accessibility

We believe theater should be for everyone, no matter what abilities or disabilities they have. All the accessibility needs and information are presented on our platform with clear icons.

Simplicity

We think that less is more, and simplicity is the most efficient way to speak with people. All the intention behind our choices is rooted in simple, clear, and easy-to-understand designs. We focus on user-friendly design and make every choice with intention, from font to layout, so everyone can find the information they need with ease.

Inclusivity

We aim to include everyone with one shared interest, a love for theater. Our mission is to make sure no one feels left out and to reflect on every person’s needs.

Welcoming Spirit

As a brand, we want to show our friendliness to our audience who wants to explore performances around the Netherlands. Our aim is to give a sense of welcoming and warm energy through our platform.

Respect

We care about our user's time, needs, and who they are. That’s why we made one easy place to find all theater shows in the Netherlands, so you don’t have to search everywhere.
